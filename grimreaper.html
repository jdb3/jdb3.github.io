Is the Grim Reaper Sick in YOUR State?


The aim of this study was to better understand how different socioeconomic factors relate to the incidence of death by Influenza and Pneumonia in the US. To make these observations, a 10-year Death Rate (number of pneumonia and influenza deaths over 10 years divided by the population) was calculated for each US State.
